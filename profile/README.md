## What is ShanXiao

Shanxiao Technology with a mission to advance learning and personal growth through innovative software development solutions. 
Inspired by the line “Looking down, all the mountains seem small” from the Chinese poem, we embrace the spirit of seeing the world from the mountain top. 
Our goal is to break traditional boundaries, providing smarter, more efficient, and convenient tools and services for users worldwide. 
Our flagship project, Canger, is a browser extension designed to help users improve their English skills anytime and anywhere, making learning a seamless part of daily life.
